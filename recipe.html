Delicious Filipino Recipes

Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add vinegar and let it boil without stirring for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add water and simmer until chicken is tender.
  5. Season with salt to taste and serve with rice.

Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened beans (red or mung beans)
  • 1/2 cup sweetened fruits (like jackfruit and banana)
  • 1/4 cup leche flan (caramel custard)
  • 1/4 cup ube (purple yam) ice cream
  • Evaporated milk to taste
  • Optional: toasted rice flakes for topping

Instructions

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans and fruits at the bottom.
  2. Add shaved ice on top of the layers.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Add leche flan and top with ube ice cream.
  5. Sprinkle with toasted rice flakes if desired, and serve immediately.

Pork Sisig

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly, boiled and chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, sauté onions and green chilies until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped pork belly and cook until crispy.
  3. Mix in mayonnaise, soy sauce, and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot on a sizzling plate.

Balut

Ingredients

  • Fertilized duck egg
  • Salt to taste
  • Vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the fertilized duck egg for about 30 minutes.
  2. Let it cool slightly before peeling.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and vinegar before eating.

Buko Pandan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young coconut, shredded
  • 1 cup pandan-flavored jelly, cubed
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded coconut and pandan jelly.
  2. Add sweetened condensed milk and coconut milk.
  3. Mix well and adjust sweetness with sugar if desired.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.

Sago't Gulaman

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sago pearls
  • 1 cup gulaman (agar-agar), cubed
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Boil sago pearls in water until translucent, then drain.
  2. In a separate pot, dissolve sugar in water and add gulaman. Boil until set.
  3. Cut gulaman into cubes and mix with sago pearls.
  4. Serve with coconut milk poured over the mixture.
